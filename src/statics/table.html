Tetra Pak has 2 divisons

  • Packaging
  • Processing

Packaging deals with all packaging material part.

Tetra Pak has 6 layers Packaging material for which 85% is paperboard, rest is food grade polyethylene and Aluminium Foil. These layers ensure: strength, shape, food quality and environmental aesthetics.

All Tetra Pak cartons are renewable and very strong table and chairs are made out of them and are supplied free of cost to NGOs.

Delhi recycles used Tetra Pak cartons into furniture, stationery for lesser privileged schools

Coming to Processing,

Tetra Pak equipment ensure that all the product going into the market for end consumer are 100% safe for consumption, as Tetra Pak brand name is involved(which we Tetra Pak has earned by hard work and keeping the consistency) they are very proactive in monitoring that all systems/equipment are working perfectly at the different client locations.

Also they upgrade sometimes few parts/modules for free to some customers so that there is not even .0001% bad product goes in to someones stomach.
